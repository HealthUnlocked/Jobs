Front-end Engineer

We're now looking for someone to transform beautiful designs and interactions into real life as HealthUnlocked moves into its next phase of growth and global expansion.

About You

Working at HealthUnlocked

We are HealthUnlocked and we are building the world’s first multi-lingual Health Graph! We connect clinicians, medical researchers and healthcare providers with patient communities across the world.

Learn more

You are a Front-end Engineer who loves building great web products for the consumer market. You think like a product guy and implement with the flair of a designer - the web should not only work, but work beautifully. You’ve already worked on a couple of web apps of various scales, and are switched on by the idea of working on a social network that is poised for massive scale. You love mobile and would embrace the opportunity of building products across platforms and devices. You probably have around...

  • 3-5 years commercial experience
  • A lifelong passion about the web, digital culture and social tech
  • Deep knowledge of front-end technologies (HTML5, CSS3 -LESS/SASS frameworks, etc.) and an interest to learn more
  • Deep knowledge of JavaScript, including one of Backbone.js, Ember.js or Angular.js (Any experience with React is a huge bonus)
  • An eye for design, UI and UX
  • Passion for building great consumer products

Email your cv, bio or portfolio to Jorge Armanet at ceo@healthunlocked.com
