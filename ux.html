Lead UX Designer

We're now looking for someone to help transform the look and feel of HealthUnlocked as we move into our next phase of growth and global expansion.

About You

Working at HealthUnlocked

We are HealthUnlocked and we are building the world’s first multi-lingual Health Graph! We connect clinicians, medical researchers and healthcare providers with patient communities across the world.

Learn more

You are a talented UX Designer who is as passionate about what you build as much as how you build it. You have already worked in an agency or consumer facing tech Startup, and now want a challenge of leading the design of rapidly growing social network with millions of current users and a clear vision to change the world for the better.

  • 3-5 years commercial experience
  • Long time passion about design, from sketching, photography and digital
  • Deep thinker about culture, values & brand
  • Student of User Centred Design
  • Lean UX-er
  • As good with people are you are perfect with pixels
  • A perfectionist who is nevertheless quick to iterate, test, secure feedback and iterate again
